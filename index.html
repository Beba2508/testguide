03

Villa Guide

Air Conditioning

Ceiling air conditioners in every room, controlled via the TOSHIBA thermostat on the wall. Use the power button and adjust temperature with + / – buttons.

Floor Heating

Each room has its own floor heating thermostat on the wall — a dial showing the current temperature. Turn the dial to set your preferred warmth.

Hot Water

Unlimited hot water from the boiler in the basement (behind the gym). Tap water is safe to drink.

Wi-Fi

Network boosters placed throughout the villa for full coverage.

NetworkVilla Lidija
Passwordlidija2023

Smart TV

Smart TVs in the living room and all three bedrooms. Press the Home button on the remote to access apps and streaming services.

Roof Blinds

Scan the QR code on the remote control, then select the direction of movement on the remote device.

Intercom

Touchscreen intercom at the entrance. Use it to communicate with visitors at the gate.

Infinity Pool

The heated infinity pool is cleaned once a week. Need extra cleaning during your stay? Just ask — no extra charge. Please supervise children at all times.

Sauna

Located in the top-floor master bedroom. Turn on using the switch outside the sauna door. Shower before use, stay hydrated, limit sessions to 10–15 minutes.

Oven

Bosch HRA574BB0. Fully equipped kitchen with hob, oven, dishwasher, fridge/freezer, coffee machine and smoothie maker.
